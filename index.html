Apple Store SQL Analysis

This project showcases advanced SQL querying techniques through the analysis of over 600k rows of Apple Store sales data, covering products, transactions, store locations, and warranty claims from retail outlets worldwide. By leveraging SQL, the analysis uncovers valuable business insights such as sales performance trends across different regions, profitability and efficiency at the store level, and patterns in warranty claims and their impact on revenue. This project demonstrates how structured queries can transform raw data into clear, actionable intelligence for strategic decision-making.
